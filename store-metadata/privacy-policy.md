# Privacy Policy for Pet Care AI

**Last Updated**: [Current Date]

## Introduction

Pet Care AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Pet Information**: Names, breeds, ages, weights, photos, and health records
- **Care Data**: Vaccination records, medications, meal schedules, and care tasks
- **Vet Information**: Veterinary clinic names and contact information
- **AI Interactions**: Questions and messages sent to the AI assistant
- **User Preferences**: App settings and notification preferences

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, screen views, interaction patterns
- **Analytics Data**: Crash reports, performance metrics, error logs
- **Location Data**: Approximate location (only when searching for emergency clinics, with your permission)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification (name, email, phone number) - unless you provide it for support
- Payment information (if using free version)
- Social media data
- Contacts or other apps on your device

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Send care reminders and notifications
- Improve AI assistant responses
- Analyze app usage and performance
- Fix bugs and improve user experience
- Provide customer support
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All pet data is stored locally on your device
- Data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- No automatic cloud backup (optional feature coming soon)

### Security Measures

- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure data transmission (HTTPS)
- Regular security audits
- No third-party data sharing without consent
